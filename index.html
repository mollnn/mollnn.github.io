Manifold Path Guiding for Importance Sampling Specular Chains
Zhimin Fan*, Pengpei Hong*, Jie Guo, Changqing Zou, Yanwen Guo, and Ling-Qi Yan
ACM Transactions on Graphics (Proceedings of SIGGRAPH ASIA 2023)
[Paper] [Supplementary] [Code]
Complex visual effects such as caustics are often produced by light paths containing multiple consecutive specular vertices (dubbed specular chains), which pose a challenge to unbiased estimation in Monte Carlo rendering. We propose a solution to importance sampling specular chains with seed placement using importance probability distributions reconstructed from historical samples. This outperforms state-of-the-art unbiased solutions with up to 40Ã— variance reduction.
